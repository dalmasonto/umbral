  1. 1

    The derive macro

    #[derive(Model)] emits impl Model, the Article::objects() manager, and the typed column module article — all of which is hand-written in src/main.rs's Article declaration alone.

  2. 2

    The migration engine

    Wrote migrations/app/0001_create_article.json at startup and applied it. SQLite assigned ids via INTEGER PRIMARY KEY AUTOINCREMENT.

  3. 3

    The templates engine

    Loaded everything under templates/ at boot. This page extends base.html and renders {{ article_count }} seeded article(s).
